Prefix m\  u\  _ 0 _ Infix

u\y has #y items resulting from applying u to each of the prefixes k{.y , for k from 1 to #y .

m\y applies successive verbs from the gerund m to the prefixes of y , extending m cyclically as required.
 		   If x>:0 , the items of x u\ y result from applying u to each infix of length x . If x<0 , u is applied to non-overlapping infixes of length |x , including any final shard.

x m\ y applies successive verbs from the gerund m to the infixes of y , extending m cyclically as required.
