5. Bordering a Table

   over=: ({.;}.)@":@,                  NB. Utility functions, intended for use
   by=:  ' '&;@,.@[,.]                  NB. rather than for immediate study

   primes=: 2 3 5
   i=: 0 1 2 3 4

   primes by i over primes */ i
