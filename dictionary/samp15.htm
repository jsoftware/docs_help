15. Junctions

Four functions are commonly used to join arguments: ; , ,. and ,: . We will illustrate them for the cases of vector and matrix arguments:
   a=: 'pqr' [ b=: 'PQR'
   m=: 3 3$ 'abcdefghi' [ n=: 3 3$ 'ABCDEFGHI'
  
   a (; ; , ; ,. ; ,:) b
