^@o. Implemented initial writing: 2009-12-03
last updated: 2010-01-19

The monad ^@o. is now implemented by special code. For example:

   ^@o. j. 0.5 * i. 3 4
1 0j1 _1 0j_1
1 0j1 _1 0j_1
1 0j1 _1 0j_1

   ^@o. j. 2e9 + 0.5 * i. 3 4
1 0j1 _1 0j_1
1 0j1 _1 0j_1
1 0j1 _1 0j_1
