i. Improved initial writing: 2007-06-19
last updated: 2007-06-23

The reflexive case of the dyad i. (i.~y and y i. y) on floating point arguments has been made faster. Likewise other verbs that depend on this case, such as ~.y and ~:y . The following benchmarks demonstrate the improvement. 

y=: 0.07 * 1e6 ?.@$ 5e5
6!:2 'Expression'
Expression      J 6.02    J 6.01    Ratio
i.~ y  0.254155 0.393665 1.55
~. y  0.261515 0.400915 1.53
~: y  0.255329 0.395172 1.55
(</. i.@#) y 0.502249 0.635284 1.26
