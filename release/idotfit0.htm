i.!.0 Improved initial writing: 2004-09-16
last updated: 2004-09-23

The dyad i.!.0 (and i:!.0) and related family of facilities on boxed arguments have been improved. The new implementation is actually somewhat slower for some arguments, but compared to before does not have arguments which give extremely poor performance.
