5!:5 and 5!:6 Improved initial writing: 2005-04-13
last updated: 2005-06-10

5!:5 and 5!:6 (linear and paren representation) have been improved for phrases involving the alphabet a. . The following examples compare the new and old results.

new:   ('abc',a.)&i.
old: (97 98 99 0 1 2 ... 254 255{a.)&i.
 
new: (12{.a.)"_
old: (a.{~i.12)"_
 
new: (_12{.a.)"_
old: (a.{~244+i.12)"_
 
new: (a. 65 66 67}~'abc')&i.
old: (0 1 2 ... 63 64 97 98 99 68 69 ... 255{a.)&i.
