Rank-0 Monads Improved initial writing: 2007-01-03
last updated: 2007-01-24

Certain rank-0 monads that compute identity functions have been improved:

B  -   boolean       + <. >. * %: | 
I  -   integer   + <. >.
X  -   extended integer  + <. >.
Q  -   rational   +
D  -   floating point   +

The following benchmarks demonstrate the improvements:

i=: 1e6 ?@$ 2e5 
b=: 1e6 ?@$ 2
