;!.f Implemented initial writing: 2007-11-27
last updated: 2007-11-27

The monad ; is now accepted by the fit conjunction ;!.f , with the fill specified by the atom f . For example:

   ;!.'_' 'DDT';(2 13$'eleemosynary');'z'

eleemosynarye
leemosynaryel
