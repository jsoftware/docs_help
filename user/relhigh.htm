J801 Release Highlights and Overview

The main change in J801 is to replace the J7 GTK IDE with one written in Qt, which has better cross-platform support.

The J801 Qt IDE also includes a new version of wd, the J Window Driver. The syntax is similar though not identical to that of J6, and is essentially a superset of the J6 functionality.

The J801 binary is the same as that in J701.

For users upgrading from J6, the J7 release notes are also relevent (replace GTK with Qt).
